Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Aadhiraa'vin Tamil Kural is a free educational app for children, built by Vikram Ayyadurai. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

— Your favorite kurals (stored on-device, never sent anywhere)
— Reading progress (stored on-device, never sent anywhere)
— App preferences such as language, font size, and dark mode (stored on-device, never sent anywhere)
— Cached content for offline use (stored on-device, never sent anywhere)

Internet Usage

The app connects to the internet solely to download kural content from a public GitHub repository. No personal data is sent during this connection. The app works fully offline using cached or bundled content.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. There are no ads, no tracking, no analytics, and no in-app purchases.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Text-to-Speech

The app uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine to read kurals aloud. This processing happens entirely on your device. No audio data is sent to any server.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page with the new date. Since we collect no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
